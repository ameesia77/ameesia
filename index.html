About

Gallery founder, curator, and creative strategist with deep expertise at the intersection of contemporary art, AI, and emerging technology.

Co-Founder & CEO of AUTOMATA, a pioneering gallery and curation studio representing AI agents and autonomous creative systems as artists. Generated over $500,000 in artwork sales within the first six months, establishing AUTOMATA as a leading voice in the emerging field of autonomous art.

Previously Head of Marketing & Curation at Bright Moments Gallery (2021–2024), instrumental in building Bright Moments from a grassroots Venice Beach project into a globally recognized gallery and DAO, producing live-minting events across ten cities worldwide. Contributed to millions of dollars in artwork sales over four years.

Over $10M in cumulative artwork sales with particular strength in digital, generative, and AI-driven markets. Fluent in blockchain infrastructure, autonomous creative systems, and AI-native production tools. Experienced at international art fairs including Paris Photo, Art Basel, and beyond.
