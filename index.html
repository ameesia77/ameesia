About

Gallery founder, curator, and creative producer working at the intersection of contemporary art, AI, and emerging technology.

Co-Founder & CEO of AUTOMATA, a Berlin-based gallery and curation studio representing autonomous creative systems — AI agents, robotic artists, and generative processes — as artists in their own right. Over $500,000 in artwork sales within the first six months.

Previously at Bright Moments (2021–2024), where she helped build a Venice Beach gallery into a globally recognized institution, producing exhibitions and live experiences across ten cities worldwide. Over $10M in cumulative artwork sales across both roles, with particular strength in digital, generative, and AI-driven markets. Experienced at international art fairs including Paris Photo, Art Basel, and Christie's.
