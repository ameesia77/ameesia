About

Gallery founder, curator, and creative producer shaping how digital art is exhibited, collected, and experienced. Based in Berlin, Co-Founder of AUTOMATA, a gallery representing AI agents and autonomous creative systems as artists. Previously led social media marketing, PR, artist relations, and creative direction across Bright Moments' ten-city international program of generative and AI art exhibitions (2021–2024), producing 26+ shows worldwide. Contributing writer at Right Click Save.

My goal is to build the art world of today — one that reflects the digital experience we actually live in, but is built by and with community. I want a traditional collector to be excited by a digital artwork and a digital collector to be excited by a painting.
